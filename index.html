shark

Sharks are a group of elasmobranch cartilaginous fishes characterized by a ribless endoskeleton, dermal denticles, five to seven gill slits on each side, and pectoral fins that are not fused to the head. Modern sharks are classified within the division Selachii and are the sister group to the Batomorphi.

hen

The chicken is a domesticated subspecies of the red junglefowl, originally native to Southeast Asia. It was first domesticated around 8,000 years ago and is one of the most common and widespread domesticated animals in the world. Chickens are primarily kept for their meat and eggs, though they are also kept as pets

ghost

"Ghost" primarily refers to the spirit of a dead person, believed by some to appear to the living, but it can also describe a haunting memory, a faint trace of something, or, in slang, the act of suddenly ending all contact with someone without explanation.
